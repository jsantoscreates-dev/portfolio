ABOUT

I'm a portuguese designer based in Lisbon, working across product design, interfaces, and graphic work. I get inspired by everything, cinema, music, history, economics, psychology, because the best design references usually have nothing to do with design.

I started in advertising and moved through graphic design, web, branding, and more recently into product and UX/UI. Not because I couldn't pick one, but because the interesting ideas live in the overlap. I've designed logistics platforms at CEVA, AI tools at Clay/KangarooTime, and campaigns for Unilever, Moche, and Fidelidade. Between all of that, freelance work in graphic design, branding, and web.

I also need to make things for myself. Small tools, posters, experiments with no brief and no deadline.

Open to product design and UX/UI roles, remote or hybrid. Also happy to take on visual design projects or build things with AI.
