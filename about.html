About

I'm a portuguese designer based in Lisbon, working across product design, interfaces, and graphic work.

I get inspired by everything, from cinema and music to history, economics, and psychology, because the best design references rarely come from design itself.

I started in advertising and moved across graphic design, web, and branding into product and UX/UI, drawn to the overlap where ideas become more interesting. I've designed logistics platforms at CEVA, AI tools at Clay and KangarooTime, and campaigns for brands like Unilever, Moche, and Fidelidade, alongside freelance work in graphic design, branding, and web.
