RELEASE

What if you could destroy the thoughts that weigh you down?

Because sometimes, to move forward, you need to see it go.

PRINTED YEARS

If posters could live outside the feed, where would you put them?

Since 2019, I've been using an Instagram page to experiment, make mistakes, and post consistently. What started as visual exercises ended up on the walls of strangers.

This gallery is my way of giving these posters a quieter space to live, outside the noise of the feed.
